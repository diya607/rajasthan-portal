E-Governance Roadmap Priorities — FY 2025–26

  • Connect all 33 district education offices to the state e-governance network by December 2025
  • Deploy offline-capable apps for areas with poor connectivity — prioritize Barmer, Jaisalmer, Balotra
  • Mandatory digital literacy training for all government college administrative staff
  • Integrate IFMS, SIPF, and SSO-Rajasthan with the education e-governance backbone
